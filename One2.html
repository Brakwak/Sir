Personal Hygiene

Why is Personal Hygiene Important?

Personal hygiene helps to prevent the spread of germs and keeps you healthy. It includes practices like washing hands, brushing teeth, and showering regularly.

Hand Washing

Wash your hands with soap and water for at least 20 seconds. This helps to remove dirt, bacteria, and viruses that can cause illness.

Oral Hygiene

Brush your teeth twice a day and floss daily. This prevents cavities, gum disease, and bad breath.

Food Hygiene

Why is Food Hygiene Important?

Food hygiene involves handling, preparing, and storing food in a way that prevents foodborne illnesses. It ensures that food is safe to eat.

Proper Food Storage

Store food at the right temperature. Refrigerate perishable foods and keep dry foods in sealed containers to prevent contamination.
